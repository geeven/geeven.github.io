time

在cocos2d-x lua开发中，经常会有各种时间相关的格式

相关列表:

格式符 说明 eg.
%a abbreviated weekday name Tue
%A full weekday name Tuesday
%b abbreviated month name May
%B full month name May
%c date and time Tue May 23 13:37:06 2017
%d day of the month 23
%H hour, 24-hour 13
%I hour,12-hour 01
%M minute 37
%m month 05
%p “AM” or “PM” PM
%S second 06
%w weekday[0-6 = Sun-Sat] 2
%x date 05/23/17
%X time 13:37:06
%Y full year 2017
%y two-digit year 17

获取当前年月日时分秒：

os.date("%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")

获取当前秒:

os.time()
